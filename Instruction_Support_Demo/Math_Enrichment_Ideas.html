## Math Enrichment Ideas - **Math in the Real World:** Have students design a simple budget for a party or event, using addition, subtraction, and multiplication to plan expenses. - **Math Poster Project:** Students create posters showing different ways to represent a number (e.g., as a fraction, decimal, and percent). - **What If? Challenge:** Ask students to change a rule in a math problem and predict how the answer would change. Discuss their reasoning as a class.
