## Language Support Strategies **Beginning:** - Offer a glossary with images for key math terms. - Use sentence starters for explanations: “The answer is greater than…” - Pair students to match math symbols with their meanings. **Intermediate:** - Provide fill-in-the-blank templates for math statements. - Guide students to restate problems in their own words. **Advanced:** - Encourage students to explain their reasoning using academic language. - Support group discussions comparing different solution methods. **Advanced High:** - Challenge students to create their own math problems and explain the solutions. - Have students review and critique sample work for accuracy and clarity.
