Use matching games to connect words with symbols. - **Step-by-Step Practice:** Guide students through simple problems before moving to more complex ones. Emphasize checking each step. - **Visual Aids:** Use number lines, charts, and drawings to help students understand concepts. --- ## Practice Worksheet **Instructions:** Solve each problem.
